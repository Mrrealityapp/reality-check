About Reality Check

Reality Check is a community-driven project dedicated to restoring trust online. Our mission is to empower people with tools to verify identities, organizations, and claims — reducing scams and protecting vulnerable communities.

Our Mission

We believe dignity and safety should be the foundation of digital interactions. By making trust visible, we help honest people be believed and protect those at risk.

Our Founder

Reality Check was founded by a disabled veteran determined to restore dignity and safety online. This project is built with compassion, persistence, and a commitment to helping vulnerable communities.
