About Reality Check

Reality Check is a community-driven project dedicated to restoring trust online. Our mission is to empower people with tools to verify identities, organizations, and claims — reducing scams and protecting vulnerable communities.

Our Mission

We believe dignity and safety should be the foundation of digital interactions. By making trust visible, we help honest people be believed and protect those at risk.

Our Founder

Reality Check was founded by a disabled veteran determined to restore dignity and safety online. This project is built with compassion, persistence, and a commitment to helping vulnerable communities.

Our Story

Reality Check was founded with a simple but urgent mission: to restore trust in digital communities. As a veteran who has seen both resilience and vulnerability firsthand, I know how critical dignity and safety are for people navigating life online.

Why We Exist

Too many individuals and organizations struggle with misinformation, scams, and broken trust. Reality Check empowers communities by verifying identities and organizations, making trust visible and protecting the vulnerable.

Our Vision

We believe in second chances, transparency, and community-driven standards. Reality Check is more than a platform — it’s a movement to ensure that everyone can believe in who they meet online.

Hands of Hope

Through our partnership with Hands of Hope, donations directly support practical help for those in need. Every contribution restores dignity and provides real-world assistance.
